Top 3D animation softwares:

Introduction:

The world of 3D design and animation is developing according to the ever increasing digital needs.If you want to design or animate something there are many websites that will help you choose between the different design software that best meets your needs.

1) Maya

Maya is widely regarded as the industry standard and offers an impressive range of tools and features. It is perfect for modeling, texturing, lighting... At the rendering level, its features include particles, hair, solid body physics, fabrics, fluid simulations and character animation.

Website: Visit autodesk.com

Pros Cons
Best at animation Really expensive 2118€/year
Great motion-capture handling node editor is cumbersome to work with
Great rendering compatibility issues

2) Cinema 4D

If you are new to the world of 3D, C4D is for you! Its intuitive operation and clean, well-laid out interface allow beginners to get good results fairly quickly.

Website: Visit maxon.net

Pros Cons
Easy to use Expensive:3480€ for a perpetual licence
Can be integrated into  After Effect. Modeling workflow not adequate
Super stable (never crash) UV editing not optimal

3) Blender

Blender is a modeling, texturing, animation and rendering application, the latest version of which offers a modern and consistent interface. It has been around for a long time and enjoys a very large community behind its continued development.

Website: Visit blender.org

Pros Cons
Free Software A bit hard to handle at first
Great for animation 3D/2D and modeling Slow viewport performance
Incredibly customizable input Bugs might hit or crash the system
