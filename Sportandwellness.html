Sport and wellness: Top 3 best apps for exercising:

Introduction:

Do you want to get back to sport? Several mobile applications for iPhone and Android smartphones can help and accompany you to achieve this noble goal without even leaving your home! Here is a selection of these applications:

1) ADIDAS RUNNING BY RUNTASTIC

This app is known to be one of the most comprehensive on the market. Indeed, it offers an interesting basis for monitoring physical efforts, whether cycling, swimming or jogging.

Website: Visit runtastic.com
