Best DNA websites IMHO:

Introduction:

Genetic genealogy laboratories offer us, through the analysis of our saliva, to reveal our ethnic origins. With the advent of genetic testing, the notion of race was definitely refuted by scientists. The idea is therefore to show us on a map the geographical areas where we come from, with potential percentages of origin.

1) 23 and Me

23andme offers to support people in the search for their ancestors and a possible quest for health. You will not only be able to understand your origins and the reasons for your difference, but you will also be able to protect yourself against serious diseases thanks to their health test.

Website: Visit 23andme.com

Pros Cons
Triangulate matches Lack of a family Tree
Relationship Match Plethora of Opt-in pages
Robust Matching Algorithm Messaging System not efficient

2) My Heritage DNA

Founded in 2003 by Gilad Japhet, MyHeritage is a paid online genealogy platform in wich Users of the platform can, among other things, know their DNA, create family trees, download and browse photos and search among billions of international historical data.

Website: Visit myheritage.com

Pros Cons
Free Upload of Other Test Small Database
Manage Multiple Kits No Chromosome Browser
Link DNA to your family tree Lack of Tools

3) Ancestry DNA

AncestryDNA is the DNA testing arm of Ancestry's genealogy service. Sending a DNA sample allows people to add DNA matches to their family tree and gain information about your predisposition to genetic diseases.

Website: Visit ancestry.co.uk

Pros Cons
Largest Database No Chromosome Browser
Linked to Tree Limited Matching Information
Genetic communities One Kit One Account
